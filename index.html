Ethics

The project 'Ethical aspects of CPS' aims to provide insights into the potential ethical concerns and related unintended impacts of the possible evolution of CPS technology by 2050. The overarching purpose is to support the European Parliament, the parliamentary bodies, and the individual Members in their anticipation of possible future concerns regarding developments in CPS, robotics and artificial intelligence.
Ethical Aspects of Cyber-Physical Systems, Scientific Foresight Report, EPRS

Overview

  • CPS include many fields
    • Robotics, IoT, computer networks, artificial intelligence, ...
  • Foresight study in 3 phases
    1. Technical horizon scan
      • envisioned developments
      • get insight into (ethical) concerns and impacts
    2. Impact analysis phase
      • analyse possible impacts and future concerns
    3. Legal backcasting phase
      • How laws need to be adapted

Future of CPS

  • We expect many benefits from future use of CPS
    • Autonomous driving, mass-customisation, telecare, smart agriculture, search-and-rescue, ...
  • Possibly many unintended impacts
    • e.g. increase of waste due to easier production
  • Impact on employment
    • Many tasks might be done by robots in the future
    • Might create many new professions
    • When is it ethical to delegate a task?

Future of CPS

  • Safety, responsibility, liability
    • How do we ensure safe cooperation between machines and humans?
    • Who is responsible in case of failure?
    • How do we prevent hacking and infiltration?
  • Privacy
    • CPS relies on vast amount of data
    • Code of conduct?
    • How do we share data?
  • Social relations
    • Robotic moral?
    • Emotional/affectionate relation between humans and machines?
    • Smart protheses => cyborgs?
